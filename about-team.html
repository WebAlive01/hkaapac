About HKA Tech

The APAC Team

The people who deliver HKA Tech's services are its greatest asset. Our APAC team combines genuine capital works delivery knowledge with deep technical expertise across PMWeb, Business Intelligence, Digital Adoption, and Advisory — operating from four offices across Australia.

Sydney Melbourne Brisbane Perth
Our People

Capital Works Knowledge, Backed by Decades of Delivery Experience

HKA Tech is part of the HKA Global organisation — the world's leading construction risk management and claims consulting firm. Our APAC team benefits directly from this connection: the people advising on PMWeb implementations, BI dashboards, and digital adoption programs are drawn from an organisation that has been managing capital works projects at every level for over 25 years.

This means our team can speak with authority not just on software configuration, but on the practical delivery challenges that capital works owners face — procurement, contract management, cost control, and program governance. We don't just theorise; we deliver practical solutions that can be implemented and deliver results.

How We Work

Software Expertise, Grounded in Project Delivery

HKA Tech's strength is the combination of technical depth and real-world capital works knowledge. Our team has been doing this since 1996. We understand both the software and the delivery context it operates in.

Practical over Theoretical

We implement and then support what we deliver. Our track record spans 300+ organisations and 25+ years of capital works technology across APAC.

Four Service Lines, One Team

Whether you need PMWeb, BI dashboards, digital adoption support, or advisory services, our team works across all four — so engagements connect seamlessly.

APAC Focused

Our team operates from Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Perth — with direct knowledge of Australian procurement frameworks, contract standards, and public sector governance.

Globally Backed

Behind our APAC team is the full weight of HKA Global — 1,000+ professionals across 45+ offices in 17 countries, with unmatched depth in capital works risk and delivery.
