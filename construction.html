Construction Management

PMWeb for Construction Managers & Head Contractors

Construction managers and head contractors across APAC use PMWeb and HKA Tech to manage multi-contract programs, control costs, track variations, and maintain the document records that protect their position throughout project delivery.

Why Construction Managers Choose PMWeb

Built for the Complexity of Multi-Contract Delivery

Construction managers operating across multiple head contracts and sub-contracts need a single system that connects cost, schedule, document management, and contract administration without creating information silos between packages. PMWeb is configured for this complexity — providing real-time visibility across all contracts, with workflows that enforce the governance disciplines that protect margin and reduce dispute exposure.

HKA Tech brings implementation experience across major construction programs in Australia and APAC — combined with HKA's unparalleled track record in construction claims and dispute resolution through Crux Insight. That depth of delivery knowledge is embedded in how we configure PMWeb for construction management organisations.

Program Delivery Visibility

Real-Time Cost and Schedule Control Across Every Contract

Construction programs that run across multiple contracts, packages, and sub-contractors can lose visibility quickly without a consolidated system. PMWeb connects cost controls, contract management, and scheduling into a single data environment — giving construction managers real-time oversight of commitments, forecasts, and variations without manual report compilation.

Multi-Contract Cost Visibility
Track budgets, commitments, variations, forecasts, and payments across all head contracts and sub-contracts — with consolidated program-level reporting available at any point.
Variation & Change Order Management
Manage change orders and variations through structured workflows — capturing the full approval chain, cost impact, and schedule effect for every change across the program.
Schedule Integration
Manage project schedules, milestones, and stage gate approvals in PMWeb — with self-contained scheduling tools that integrate directly into cost and workflow modules.

PMWeb Modules for Construction Managers

The Platform Capabilities Most Used by Construction Management Organisations

PMWeb is a configurable platform — construction managers typically rely on this set of modules across the delivery lifecycle, from contract administration and cost control through to document management and handover.

Contract Management

Comprehensive contract management for head contracts and sub-contracts — with direct integration into cost, schedule, and workflow modules supporting variations, notices, and payment claims in a single system.

Cost Controls

Manage and track budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices, and payments across all packages and programs — with real-time variance reporting and complete audit trail.

Document Control

Centralised document management with version control, transmittal tracking, and direct Bluebeam integration — managing drawings, specifications, RFIs, submittals, and project records throughout delivery.

Workflow & Approvals

Drag-and-drop document routing and configurable approval workflows — ensuring every RFI, variation, payment claim, and submittal follows the correct approval path with a full audit trail.

Scheduling & Stage Gates

Manage project schedules, milestones, and stage gate approvals — with self-contained scheduling tools and full integration into cost and contract management for end-to-end delivery control.

Dashboards & Reports

Configurable project and program dashboards with drill-down to underlying data — for owner reporting, internal program reviews, and executive briefings across the construction portfolio.

Field Mobility

True zero-footprint mobile access — run PMWeb from any browser on iPad, iPhone, tablet, or smartphone. Field teams can submit RFIs, update site records, and progress workflows without returning to the office.

Integration & Interfaces

Connect PMWeb with Bluebeam, BIM platforms, ERP systems, and other enterprise applications via API and SSIS — eliminating duplicate data entry across the construction technology stack.

Contract & Risk Management

Protecting Your Position Through Every Stage of Delivery

Construction managers face significant commercial risk across complex, multi-party contract environments. PMWeb maintains a complete, structured record of every contract notice, variation, payment claim, and approval decision — reducing the information gaps that escalate into disputes and providing the audit trail that supports commercial resolution when issues arise.

Complete Contractual Record
Every notice, variation, claim, and approval is recorded with user, timestamp, and status — creating the contemporaneous record that protects your position in any dispute or audit scenario.
Risk Register Integration
Manage project and program risk registers in PMWeb — linked directly to cost contingency, contract provisions, and the approvals process for risk mitigation actions.
Payment Claim Management
Track progress claims, payment schedules, and retention through structured workflows — reducing payment disputes and maintaining cash flow visibility across the full sub-contract package.
