Retail & Banking Capital Works

PMWeb for Retail & Banking Property Programs

Major retailers and financial institutions managing large property portfolios rely on PMWeb and HKA Tech to deliver store rollouts, branch refurbishments, and capital programs with the speed, cost discipline, and reporting consistency that portfolio-scale delivery demands.

Discuss Your Requirements Explore PMWeb
Why Retail & Banking Chooses PMWeb

Purpose-Built for High-Volume, Multi-Site Capital Delivery

Retail chains and financial institutions face a capital works challenge that is fundamentally different from other sectors — dozens or hundreds of concurrent fitouts and refurbishments, each with tight time-to-trade deadlines, standardised specifications, and the commercial pressure of trading continuity. General project management tools cannot handle the volume, the reporting consolidation, or the procurement complexity at scale.

PMWeb provides a configurable platform purpose-built for owners and program managers of multi-site property portfolios — delivering consistent process, cost control, and real-time visibility across every store or branch in the capital works program.

Portfolio-Scale Visibility

One View Across Every Store, Branch, and Fitout in Progress

Managing 20, 50, or 200 concurrent store fitouts requires a consolidated program view — not a spreadsheet per site. PMWeb delivers a single source of truth across all projects in the rollout program, with real-time cost, schedule, and status visibility from individual site level up to executive portfolio dashboards.

Program-Level Dashboards
Roll up status, cost variance, and schedule performance from every site into a single portfolio view — with drill-down to individual project data without manual report compilation.
Standardised Project Templates
Deploy consistent cost structures, approval workflows, and document templates across every site in the rollout — reducing setup time and ensuring program-wide reporting consistency.
Time-to-Trade Tracking
Monitor milestone progress, practical completion, and handover dates across all sites — with schedule variance alerts that flag at-risk openings before they become trading losses.

PMWeb Modules for Retail & Banking

The Platform Capabilities Most Used by Retail & Banking Clients

PMWeb is a configurable platform — retail chains and financial institutions typically rely on this set of modules to manage store rollouts, branch refurbishments, and ongoing property capital programs.

Cost Controls

Manage and track budgets, contracts, change orders, forecasts, and payments across every site in the rollout program — with consolidated cost reporting at portfolio level.

Contract Management

Comprehensive contract management with direct integration into cost, schedule, and workflow — supporting head contracts, fitout contractors, and variation management across all sites.

Workflow & Approvals

Configurable approval workflows for invoices, variations, and design sign-offs — routing documents through defined chains aligned to your organisation's authority levels.

Scheduling & Stage Gates

Manage fitout programs, milestone dates, and practical completion sign-offs — with stage gate approvals tracking handover readiness and time-to-trade milestones across the rollout.

Dashboards & Reports

Configurable executive dashboards delivering program-level views of spend, schedule, and delivery status — built for property directors, finance teams, and board-level capital reporting.

Document Control

Centralised document management with version control and transmittal tracking — for design drawings, fitout specifications, lease documentation, and project records across all sites.

Procurement Management

Manage panel contractor sourcing, tender evaluation, and vendor selection — supporting multi-site rollout procurement with consistent process and consolidated supplier performance tracking.

Asset Management

Track, monitor, and maintain store fixtures, equipment, and building components — with preventive maintenance, work orders, and lifecycle cost visibility across the property portfolio.

Cost Discipline at Scale

Consistent Cost Control Across Every Site in the Program

For a retailer or bank running 50 concurrent fitouts, a 5% cost overrun on each site compounds quickly into a material program-level variance. PMWeb enforces consistent cost control disciplines across every site — with the same budget structures, variation controls, and payment workflows applied at scale, not managed differently site by site.

Standardised Budget Structures
Deploy the same cost breakdown structure across every site in the rollout — enabling like-for-like performance comparison and aggregated portfolio cost reporting at any point in the program.
Variation & Change Order Control
All contractor variations are captured, evaluated, and approved through defined workflows before cost impact is recognised — preventing scope creep from eroding program budgets.
Forecast-to-Budget Visibility
Real-time forecast tracking against approved budget at both site and program level — giving property and finance teams the early warning signals needed to manage capital expenditure within board-approved envelopes.
