Hosting Options for Every Organisation

PMWeb is available via Amazon Web Services SaaS — HKA Tech's primary and recommended deployment model. Managed hosting and on-premise options are available where specific data sovereignty, security, or infrastructure requirements apply.

Primary — Recommended
AWS SaaS

PMWeb delivered via Amazon Web Services — fully managed, scalable, and maintained by HKA Tech. No server infrastructure for your organisation to manage.
