Change Management

The HKA Change Methodology — Enacting Lasting Change

Organisational Change Management (OCM) is the process of enacting positive change within an organisation. It involves identifying the reason for change, the involved parties, and effective communication across every level of the program office.

At HKA Tech, we believe effective OCM is key to a successful capital works technology implementation. Our Change Methodology addresses five key elements:

  • Vision and Skills Creating a clear picture of where the organisation is heading and identifying the internal and external capabilities needed to deliver it.
  • Incentives and Resources Defining both positive and negative incentives for adoption, and ensuring the right people, tools, and budget are allocated to sustain change.
  • Action Plan A comprehensive plan from start to finish — including communications, training, and a staged rollout aligned to your program's governance framework.
