Education Capital Works

PMWeb for Education Infrastructure & Capital Programs

Universities, schools, and TAFE institutions across APAC rely on PMWeb and HKA Tech to manage campus builds, long-range capital programs, and facility assets — with the funding accountability, governance, and portfolio visibility that education sector delivery demands.

Discuss Your Requirements Explore PMWeb
Why Education Chooses PMWeb

Built for the Complexity of Campus Capital Programs

Education organisations don't run one project — they manage rolling capital programs across campuses, often funded by multiple sources with strict accountability requirements. PMWeb provides the single system of record that keeps programs on track from feasibility through to facility management.

Multi-Stage Program Planning

Manage master planning, stage-gate approvals, and long-range capital programs across multiple campuses — with full budget visibility at every level of the WBS.

Funding & Budget Accountability

Track government grants, institutional funding, and donor contributions with transparent cost reporting that satisfies governing bodies, audit requirements, and state funding agencies.

Campus Asset & Facility Management

Transition from construction to operations without losing continuity. Manage buildings, facilities, and infrastructure assets through the full asset lifecycle on one platform.

Cost Controls & Funding

Full Visibility Over Every Dollar Across Your Capital Program

Education capital programs typically operate under tight funding conditions — government appropriations, donor commitments, and institutional reserves all flowing into complex multi-stage builds. PMWeb tracks all funding sources, budget allocations, contract commitments, and expenditure in real time, ensuring you can report accurately at any point in the program lifecycle.

  • Fund source tracking across government grants, institutional, and donor contributions
  • Budget control at program, project, and WBS level with contingency management
  • Contract variation management with automated cost-to-complete forecasting
  • Approval workflows aligned to institutional delegation authorities
  • Audit-ready reporting for governing bodies and state funding agencies

Document & Data Control

One Source of Truth for Every Drawing, Specification, and Approval

Campus construction involves hundreds of stakeholders — architects, structural engineers, fit-out contractors, facility managers, and institutional project teams — each generating and consuming documentation at volume. PMWeb's document management module provides version-controlled storage, structured review workflows, and full audit trails across every project document from design through to handover.

  • Version-controlled document register for drawings, specifications, and reports
  • Structured review and approval workflows with email notifications
  • Bluebeam integration for mark-up and review of construction drawings
  • Document transmittals with full issue and receipt tracking
  • Handover documentation package compiled automatically at practical completion

Operate

Manage Your Campus Facilities and Assets After Construction Closes Out

For education organisations, construction is only the beginning. Once a building is handed over, it needs to be maintained, renewed, and eventually replaced — across a portfolio that can span dozens of buildings and decades of life. PMWeb's Operate phase capabilities let you manage preventive maintenance schedules, work orders, and long-range asset renewal planning from the same system you used to build the facility.

  • Campus asset hierarchy — buildings, floors, spaces, and equipment in one register
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance scheduling with work order management
  • Condition assessment tracking to support long-range renewal planning
  • Lease management for rented premises and commercial tenancies on campus
  • Capital renewal forecasting linked back to original project cost data
