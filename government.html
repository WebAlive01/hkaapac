Government Capital Works

PMWeb for Government Infrastructure & Capital Programs

State and local government agencies across APAC rely on PMWeb and HKA Tech to manage buildings, infrastructure, and capital works programs — with the compliance, governance, and visibility that public sector delivery demands.

Discuss Your Requirements Explore PMWeb
Why Government Chooses PMWeb

Built for the Complexity of Public Sector Capital Delivery

Government capital programs operate under procurement rules, audit obligations, and public accountability frameworks that general project management software cannot accommodate. PMWeb is the specialist platform for owners and managers of public infrastructure — configurable to align with Australian Government frameworks, state procurement standards, and the full Plan to Operate lifecycle.

HKA Tech has more government PMWeb deployments in APAC than any other implementation partner — across transport authorities, health departments, education bodies, and local councils.

Portfolio Visibility

Real-Time Oversight Across Every Project and Program

Government agencies managing multiple concurrent capital works programs need a single source of truth — across funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, and project status. PMWeb provides consolidated portfolio dashboards that roll up from individual project data in real time, without manual report compilation.

Multi-Program Portfolio Management
Manage portfolios, programs, and projects in a single configurable system — with drill-down from executive dashboards to underlying cost and schedule data.
Configurable Approval Workflows
Route documents, change orders, and invoices through defined approval chains — fully auditable and aligned to your agency's governance structure.
Funding & Budget Tracking
Track funding sources, budget allocations, forecasts, and expenditure at every level of the WBS — with real-time variance reporting across all projects.

PMWeb Modules for Government

The Platform Capabilities Most Used by Government Clients

PMWeb is a configurable platform — government agencies typically rely on this set of modules across the capital works lifecycle, from early program planning through construction delivery and ongoing asset management.

Cost Controls

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices, and payments across all projects and programs — with complete audit trail.

Contract Management

Comprehensive contract management with direct integration into cost, schedule, and workflow — supporting head contracts, sub-contracts, and variations in a single system.

Workflow & Approvals

Drag-and-drop document routing and configurable approval workflows — ensuring every submission, RFI, and payment claim follows your agency's defined governance process.

Document Control

Centralised document management with version control, transmittal tracking, and direct Bluebeam integration — for drawings, specifications, and project records.

Scheduling & Stage Gates

Manage project schedules, milestones, and stage gate approvals — with self-contained scheduling tools and full integration into the cost and workflow modules.

Dashboards & Reports

Configurable executive dashboards with drill-down to underlying project data — built for ministerial reporting, board briefings, and internal program reviews.

Procurement Management

Manage sourcing, tender evaluation, and vendor selection through structured procurement workflows — supporting compliance with government procurement frameworks and panel arrangements.

Asset Management

Track, monitor, and maintain government facilities, equipment, and infrastructure components — with preventive maintenance, work orders, and lifecycle cost visibility.

Compliance & Governance

Audit-Ready Records for Every Project Decision

Government capital works programs are subject to audit at any point — by the Auditor-General, parliamentary committees, or internal governance bodies. PMWeb maintains a complete, immutable record of every cost movement, approval decision, contract variation, and document revision across the program lifecycle.

Complete Audit Trail
Every transaction, approval, and document change is recorded with user, timestamp, and action — providing a defensible record for audit and parliamentary scrutiny.
Panel & Procurement Compliance
PMWeb supports government panel arrangements, coordinated procurement, and standing offer compliance across state and Commonwealth frameworks.
Data Sovereignty & Hosting Options
Deployed on AWS with Australian data residency options, or as a managed or on-premise solution — meeting agency data classification and sovereignty requirements.
