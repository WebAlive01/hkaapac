Cost Controls

Full Visibility Across Every Budget, Contract & Change

PMWeb's cost control capabilities give owners a single, authoritative view of all project and program expenditure — from original budget through to final account. Funding, commitments, variations, risks, and forecasts are tracked in real time and presented in dashboards that reflect the actual state of delivery.

Every cost event — from an approved variation to a contractor invoice — is captured in PMWeb and immediately visible across your portfolio reports and dashboards.

  • Budget and funding managementTrack funding sources, approved budgets, and contingency at every WBS level across the portfolio — with real-time rollup to program level.
  • Change order managementFull change order lifecycle — request, review, approval, and cost impact — integrated with contracts and forecasts with complete audit trail.
  • Invoice and payment trackingProgress claims, payment certificates, and retention management across all contractors — with configurable approval workflows.

Contract Management

Contract Management Integrated Across the Whole Platform

PMWeb's contract management features are not a standalone module — they are deeply integrated with cost, schedule, workflow, dashboards, and reporting. Every contract, variation, subcontract, and payment certificate connects directly to the project's financial picture.

For government and institutional owners, this integration provides the real-time contract status and audit trail required for governance reporting and treasury accountability.

  • Contract registerMaintain a complete record of all contracts and their current status, approved value, and full variation history — linked directly to cost control.
  • Variation and change trackingManage requests for information, potential changes, and approved variations with full audit trail from identification through to cost impact.
  • Subcontract managementExtend contract tracking down through contractor and subcontractor tiers, with consistent reporting at every level.

Program & Project Management

Portfolios, Programs & Projects Managed in One Place

PMWeb provides comprehensive tools for managing capital works at every level — from individual projects through to multi-agency portfolios. Schedule management, stage gates, and reporting are configurable to your organisation's governance framework.

  • Portfolio and program dashboardsReal-time rollup of schedule status, cost performance, and risk across every project in delivery — drill down to individual tasks from portfolio level.
  • Stage gates and workflowConfigurable approval gates with automated document routing ensure delivery phases are reviewed before proceeding — with a complete approval audit trail.
  • Framework alignmentConfigurable to PMBOK, PRINCE2, or your organisation's own project management methodology — no rework required during implementation.
