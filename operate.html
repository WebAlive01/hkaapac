The Operate Phase

Asset Management, Leases & Ongoing Operations

Most project management platforms stop at practical completion. PMWeb doesn't. The Operate phase delivers a fully integrated environment for asset tracking, preventive maintenance, lease management, and facility operations — all connected to the cost and document data built during Plan and Build.

Track, monitor, map, and assess locations, facilities, equipment, and subcomponents — with preventive and predictive maintenance, work requests, and work orders.

Lease Management

Work Orders & Requests

Receive, prioritise, and assign work requests from any location. Track work orders to resolution with full audit trails, cost capture, and integration with your asset register.

Asset Management

A Complete Register of Every Asset You Own

PMWeb gives you a single, structured asset register that links directly to the project records created during the Build phase. Every asset — building, piece of infrastructure, plant, or equipment — is registered with its full hierarchy, location data, condition status, and maintenance history.

Preventive maintenance schedules are configured at the asset level and automatically generate work orders on the required cycle. Predictive maintenance rules can be applied based on usage, age, or condition thresholds — ensuring your maintenance program is responsive, not just reactive.

  • Asset hierarchiesUnlimited depth — from precinct to individual component — with location mapping linking assets to specific buildings, floors, and spaces.
  • Maintenance schedulingAutomatic work order generation on preventive cycles, with condition assessments recording inspection results against defined rating scales.
  • Whole-of-life cost captureEvery work order cost is captured against the relevant asset, enabling accurate whole-of-life cost analysis and capital renewal forecasting.

Lease Management

Sophisticated Lease Administration, Fully Integrated

For organisations that own and lease property — government agencies managing tenanted buildings, healthcare organisations with commercial leases, developers with income-producing assets — PMWeb's Lease Management module provides a complete administration framework.

Lease abstracts capture all key terms. Charge calculations handle rent, outgoings, CPI adjustments, and incentives. Suite linking connects each tenancy to the physical space in your asset register. All lease financials flow directly into your cost and portfolio dashboards.

  • Lease abstractsCapture all key commercial terms — rent, outgoings, CPI adjustments, incentives, and renewal options — in a structured, searchable format.
  • Charge calculations and suite linkingSophisticated charge logic connects each tenancy to specific spaces in the asset register, with all financials flowing to your portfolio dashboards.
  • Workflow integrationLease approvals, renewals, and variations are managed through configurable PMWeb workflows — maintaining a complete audit trail for compliance.

Work Orders & Facility Management

From Work Request to Resolution, in One System

PMWeb handles the full work order lifecycle — from the initial request raised by a facility user through to assignment, scheduling, execution, and sign-off. Every step is tracked, costed, and connected to the relevant asset record.

Because work order data sits alongside your asset register, cost records, and document management, you always have a complete picture of what work has been done, what it cost, and what impact it has on the projected life of your assets.

  • Work request submissionFrom any device — browser-based, zero footprint required. Priority classification and routing rules apply automatically based on asset type and urgency.
  • Contractor managementPurchase order integration for external works, with time and materials capture against each work order for accurate job costing.
  • Approval and escalation workflowsConfigurable workflows manage approvals, escalations, and completion sign-off — with a full audit trail for compliance and reporting.
