What We Do

Capital Works Technology, Delivered by People Who Understand Projects

HKA Tech is our technology group that focuses on delivery of the PMWeb software solution for management of capital works projects throughout their lifecycle – from concept, through delivery, to long-term asset management. HKA Tech is the largest implementing reseller of PMWeb globally.

HKA Tech is in the unique position of not only providing PMWeb software and implementing the solution; but also being part of the global HKA organisation that is a recognised leader in the management of capital works projects.

HKA maintains its own PMWeb facility — originally located in a secure Equinix premium data centre and now in the Microsoft Azure Cloud — delivering PMWeb controls over the Internet, to our project delivery teams, anywhere in the world. We can therefore speak with a great deal of experience when advising on not only the product itself and how to go about implementing it; but also the detail of running programs, projects and contracts in PMWeb on a daily basis.

We keep our clients' business interests and directions in mind, challenging the norm and offering and implementing innovative solutions. All of our work is underpinned by solid project management principles. We don't just theorise; we deliver practical solutions that can be implemented and deliver results – making the best possible outcome a reality, every time.

Our Journey

From PCI Group to HKA Tech — A History of Capital Works Technology

1996
PCI Group Founded

HKA Tech's origins date back to the formation of PCI Group in 1996 and the development of its expertise in construction industry software solutions.

2006–2008
#1 Global Dealer for Prolog

PCI Group was the #1 global dealer for Prolog, implementing project control solutions and training clients in the use of both Prolog and Primavera.

2008
Acquired by Hill International

PCI Group was acquired by Hill in 2008, continuing its focus on construction industry software solutions and project control technologies as Hill PCI.

2011
PMWeb Partnership Begins

Hill PCI became a dealer for PMWeb in 2011, as the product was rapidly gaining recognition as a leading software solution for managing capital works projects.

2013
World's Largest PMWeb Implementer

By 2013, Hill PCI had become the world's largest implementing reseller for PMWeb — a position it maintains to this day as HKA Tech.

2016
Acquired by Bridgepoint

In December 2016, the construction claims group of Hill International, Inc was acquired by Bridgepoint Development Capital (BDC), part of Bridgepoint, an international private equity group headquartered in London, United Kingdom.

2022
PAI Partners Ownership

Bridgepoint's ownership was acquired by PAI Partners — one of the top five private equity firms in France, with origins dating back to 1872 and €30+ billion assets under management.
