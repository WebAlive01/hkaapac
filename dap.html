Digital Adoption

WalkMe Implementation and Digital Adoption Services for APAC

Software is only valuable when your people use it effectively. HKA Tech designs and deploys digital adoption solutions that drive real system proficiency — for PMWeb and enterprise applications across the Asia Pacific region.

What Is Digital Adoption

Maximise the Return on Your System Investment

Deploying enterprise software is the starting point, not the finish line. Without structured adoption, even the best-configured system fails to deliver its potential — as users revert to familiar workarounds, training gains erode, and system data quality deteriorates. HKA Tech's Digital Adoption Platform services close the gap between deployment and proficiency through intelligent, in-application guidance that reaches users exactly when they need it.

WalkMe Expertise

In-Application Guidance That Drives Proficiency

HKA Tech is a WalkMe Certified Deployment Partner — one of a small number of certified implementers in the Asia Pacific region. Our implementations go beyond switching on the platform: we design the guidance content, map the user journeys, configure the workflows, and measure the outcomes.

  • Step-by-step walkthroughs that guide users through complex processes without leaving the application
  • Smart tips and alerts that surface contextual help at the precise moment of need
  • Automation of repetitive tasks to reduce data entry errors and accelerate workflows
  • Adoption analytics that show where users struggle, which features are underused, and where guidance needs strengthening
  • Segmented experiences so different user roles receive guidance tailored to their specific tasks and permissions

Adoption Analytics

Measure Adoption — and Act on What You Find

Adoption is not a one-time activity. It requires ongoing monitoring, iteration, and refinement as systems evolve and user behaviours shift. WalkMe's analytics layer — configured and interpreted by HKA Tech — provides a continuous feedback loop that keeps your adoption investment productive well beyond go-live.

  • Completion rate tracking for every walkthrough and step sequence across your user base
  • Drop-off analysis that identifies exactly where users abandon a process — enabling targeted guidance improvements
  • Feature engagement reporting showing which platform capabilities remain underutilised
  • User segment comparison to identify adoption gaps between teams, regions, or role types

Beyond PMWeb

Digital Adoption Across Your Enterprise

HKA Tech's DAP services extend well beyond PMWeb. Any enterprise application your teams use can benefit from structured, in-application guidance — reducing training time, improving data quality, and accelerating system value.

PMWeb

Our core deployment environment. WalkMe guidance for cost management, document control, workflow approvals, and reporting — configured to your organisation's specific PMWeb implementation.

ERP & Finance Systems

In-application walkthroughs for SAP, Oracle, and other ERP environments — supporting complex procurement and financial processes used by capital works teams.

GIS & Asset Platforms

Guidance layers for geographic information systems and asset management platforms used by transport, utilities, and government infrastructure teams.

Procurement & Tendering

Step-by-step guidance for complex tendering and evaluation workflows — reducing errors in procurement processes where compliance and auditability are critical.

Document Management

In-application guidance for document management systems — helping teams adhere to naming conventions, approval routing, and version control requirements.

HR & Workforce Systems

Onboarding walkthroughs and ongoing guidance for HR platforms used by large capital works organisations managing complex workforce and contractor environments.

Accessibility

WCAG Compliance with UserWay

HKA Tech also supports the implementation of UserWay's accessibility platform — helping capital works organisations meet their obligations under WCAG 2.1 and government accessibility standards across their digital estate.

  • AI-powered accessibility engine that remediates common WCAG issues automatically, without code changes to the underlying application
  • Accessibility widget that allows end users to adjust contrast, font size, spacing, and other display settings to suit their individual needs
  • Screen reader optimisation and keyboard navigation improvements for enterprise web applications
  • Accessibility reporting providing an audit trail for compliance obligations
