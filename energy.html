Energy & Resources Capital Works

PMWeb for Energy & Resources Infrastructure Programs

Utilities, energy generators, network operators, and resources companies across APAC rely on PMWeb and HKA Tech to manage capital programs — delivering visibility across large-scale asset builds, maintenance capital, and sustaining works with the cost control and schedule discipline that complex energy programs demand.

Discuss Your Requirements Explore PMWeb
Why Energy & Resources Chooses PMWeb

Built for the Scale and Complexity of Energy Infrastructure

Energy and resources capital programs operate across multiple concurrent projects — generation, transmission, distribution, pipelines, and sustaining capital — under strict regulatory reporting, contractor management, and safety compliance requirements. General project management software cannot support the cost control depth, asset lifecycle management, or multi-program portfolio visibility that these programs demand.

PMWeb gives energy and resources organisations a single configurable platform that spans the full capital lifecycle — from early-stage feasibility and estimating through construction delivery and into long-term asset operation and maintenance. HKA Tech brings implementation experience across utilities, transmission networks, pipelines, and resources projects across APAC.

Program-Level Visibility

One View Across Every Capital Program and Asset Build

Energy and resources programs typically run multiple major projects concurrently — each with distinct contractors, funding sources, and delivery timelines. PMWeb consolidates portfolio-level visibility in real time, rolling up from individual project cost data to program dashboards without manual report compilation or spreadsheet aggregation.

Multi-Project Portfolio Management
Manage sustaining capital, major builds, and regulatory upgrade programs in a single system — with drill-down from executive dashboards to individual cost items and contracts.
Cost Control & Forecasting
Track approved budgets, committed costs, actuals, and forecasts at every level of the WBS — with real-time variance reporting and AI-assisted cost forecasting in PMWeb 8.0.
Contractor & Contract Management
Manage head contracts, sub-contracts, variations, and payment claims across a complex contractor ecosystem — with full integration into cost, schedule, and approval workflows.

PMWeb Modules for Energy & Resources

The Platform Capabilities Most Used by Energy & Resources Clients

PMWeb is a configurable platform — energy and resources organisations typically rely on this set of modules across the capital works lifecycle, from feasibility and contract award through construction delivery and ongoing asset operations.

Cost Controls

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices, and payments across all projects and programs — with complete cost history and variance reporting.

Contract Management

Comprehensive contract management with direct integration into cost, schedule, and workflow — supporting head contracts, sub-contracts, and variations for complex contractor arrangements.

Asset Management

Track, monitor, and maintain energy infrastructure assets — generation plant, transmission network assets, pipelines, and substations — with preventive maintenance, work orders, and lifecycle cost visibility.

Workflow & Approvals

Drag-and-drop document routing and configurable approval workflows — ensuring every change order, payment claim, and procurement decision follows your organisation's defined governance process.

Dashboards & Reports

Configurable executive dashboards with drill-down to underlying project data — built for board reporting, regulatory submissions, and internal program performance reviews.

Document Control

Centralised document management with version control, transmittal tracking, and direct Bluebeam integration — for engineering drawings, specifications, safety documentation, and project records.

Scheduling & Stage Gates

Manage project schedules, milestones, and stage gate approvals — with self-contained scheduling tools and full integration into cost and workflow modules across the capital program lifecycle.

Risk Management

Identify, assess, and track project and program risks — with direct linkage to cost contingency, mitigation actions, and executive risk dashboards across the energy capital portfolio.

Asset Lifecycle Management

From Capital Build to Operational Asset — in One System

Energy and resources organisations invest heavily in capital programs that ultimately become operational assets. PMWeb spans the full lifecycle — from estimating and procurement through construction delivery into the Operate phase — so the asset data created during capital delivery flows directly into ongoing maintenance and operations management without re-entry or system change.

Sustaining Capital Management
Manage sustaining capital and maintenance programs alongside major builds — with consistent cost control, approval workflows, and reporting across all capital spend categories.
Preventive & Predictive Maintenance
PMWeb's asset management module supports preventive maintenance scheduling, work requests, and work orders — keeping energy infrastructure assets operating within regulatory and safety requirements.
Regulatory & Safety Compliance
Maintain complete, auditable records for every project decision, safety approval, and asset modification — supporting regulatory reporting, safety case documentation, and compliance audits.
