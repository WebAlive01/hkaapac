PMWeb Platform

Every Feature Capital Works
Organisations Need

PMWeb covers the full capital works lifecycle — from program set-up and estimating through construction delivery to asset management and operations. A single configurable platform, built specifically for owners of buildings and infrastructure.

Full Lifecycle Coverage

One Platform. Plan, Build, and Operate.

PMWeb is not a general-purpose project management tool. It is purpose-built for capital works owners — structured around the way buildings, infrastructure, and capital programs are actually planned, delivered, and managed over their full life.

Lifecycle — Phase 01

Plan

Define scope, set baselines, establish governance structures, and bring every project in a program to a state of readiness before a single dollar of construction expenditure is committed.

Plan

Planning & Program Set-Up

PMWeb enables owners to define the full scope of a capital program from the top down — establishing stage gates, approval workflows, governance hierarchies, and project charters before the first contractor is engaged. Every project starts with a structured baseline, not a blank spreadsheet.

  • Program and portfolio hierarchy — phases, packages, projects
  • Stage gate approvals with configurable workflow routing
  • Business case and funding approval tracking
  • Risk register and risk response planning

Plan

Estimating & Budgeting

Set program budgets, resource cost estimates, and contingency at every level of the Work Breakdown Structure from day one. PMWeb's estimating tools allow owners to build cost plans that flow automatically into cost control once the project enters delivery — eliminating the manual re-entry that causes forecast errors on large programs.

  • Multi-level WBS cost planning with contingency management
  • Funding source and allocation tracking across the program
  • Estimate templates and historical cost benchmarks
  • Direct connection to cost control on project commencement

Procurement

Manage sourcing, tender evaluation, and vendor selection through structured procurement workflows — fully integrated with contract management so the transition from tender to awarded contract is seamless. All documents, evaluations, and approvals are captured in a single audit trail.

  • Sourcing and expression of interest management
  • Tender evaluation scoring with configurable criteria
  • Approval workflow for contract award and execution
  • Direct handover to contract management on award

Lifecycle — Phase 02

Build

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, schedule, documents, and field activities across every project — in real time, from any device.

Build

Cost Controls

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices and payments across all projects and programs. PMWeb's cost control module provides a single source of financial truth — eliminating the parallel spreadsheet systems that cause budget surprises on major capital programs.

  • Budget tracking against original, approved, and forecast values
  • Change order management with full approval trail
  • Invoice and payment processing with cash flow forecasting
  • Contingency drawdown and risk event tracking

Contract Management

Extensive contract management features that dovetail into all areas of PMWeb — cost, schedule, workflow, dashboards and reports in real time. Manage head contracts, subcontracts, and supply agreements from a single location, with every variation, claim, and payment certificate tracked to the source document.

  • Head contracts, subcontracts, and supply agreements
  • Variations and extensions of time with approval workflow
  • Progress claims, payment certificates, and retention management
  • Defect lists and practical completion checklists

Build

Program & Project Management

Comprehensive tools for portfolios, programs and projects — configurable to align with PMBOK, PRINCE2 or your organisation's preferred framework. Manage schedules, issues, risks, meetings, and correspondence from a single platform, with full version control on all project documents.

  • Scheduling and milestone tracking across programs
  • Issue, risk, and action item registers with escalation rules
  • Correspondence management and meeting minutes
  • Configurable to PMBOK, PRINCE2, or custom frameworks

Build

Mobility

True zero-footprint access — run PMWeb from any browser on iPad, iPhone, tablet or smartphone with no downloads required. Field teams can raise issues, update progress, sign off on documents, and complete inspections from the construction site, with all data synchronised in real time to the central platform.

  • Browser-based — no app installation required on any device
  • Field inspection forms, photo capture, and sign-off
  • Real-time synchronisation with central project data
  • Responsive interface designed for touch and stylus input

Lifecycle — Phase 03

Operate

Once a project reaches practical completion, PMWeb doesn't stop. Asset management, facility management, and lease management tools keep the same platform working for the full operational life of your assets.

Operate

Asset Management

Track, monitor, map and assess locations, facilities, equipment and their subcomponents — with preventive and predictive maintenance, work requests and work orders. PMWeb's asset management module is designed for organisations that need to manage the ongoing condition and performance of built assets at scale.

  • Asset register with location hierarchy and condition tracking
  • Preventive maintenance schedules and work order management
  • Work requests and fault reporting from field teams
  • Integration with capital works data from the Build phase

Lease Management

Define and manage cost and revenue leases with sophisticated charge calculations, lease abstracts, suite linking, and direct integration with planning and workflow. Suitable for organisations managing commercial, retail, community, or mixed-use property portfolios alongside their capital works programs.

  • Cost and revenue lease management with charge calculations
  • Lease abstracts, suite linking, and tenancy register
  • Rent review tracking and option expiry management
  • Integration with operational workflow and approval processes
