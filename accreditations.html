As PMWeb's authorised implementation partner for the Asia Pacific region, HKA Tech holds the highest level of certification available to regional partners — covering configuration, data migration, integrations, training, and ongoing support.

Government Procurement

Registered on Key Government Panels

HKA Tech holds active registrations on government procurement panels across Australian jurisdictions — enabling state and local government agencies to engage us under established supplier arrangements without separate tender processes.

New South Wales
Local Government Procurement (LGP)

HKA Tech is registered on the NSW Local Government Procurement panel — enabling NSW councils and local government organisations to procure PMWeb implementation, Business Intelligence, and Digital Adoption services directly under the LGP supplier arrangement.

Queensland
QAssure

Registered on the Queensland Government's QAssure ICT supplier panel, HKA Tech can be engaged directly by Queensland government agencies for software, implementation, and advisory services across capital works programs.

Victoria
Victorian eServices Register

HKA Tech holds active registration on the Victorian Government's eServices Register, enabling Victorian state and local government entities to engage our technology and advisory services under established panel terms.

South Australia
SA eProjects Plus

Registered on SA eProjects Plus, HKA Tech can deliver project management technology, implementation services, and advisory support to South Australian government agencies under the state government's established ICT procurement framework.

Panel registrations are reviewed and maintained annually. Contact us to confirm current panel availability for your jurisdiction.
