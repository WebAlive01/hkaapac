Our Story

People Who Understand How Projects Are Built

HKA Tech APAC has spent over 25 years implementing, configuring, and supporting PMWeb for organisations managing some of the region's most complex capital works programs. We are not a general IT firm that happens to sell project management software — we are capital works specialists who bring hands-on delivery knowledge alongside deep technical capability.

Our team has direct experience across government, infrastructure, healthcare, education, energy, and property — the sectors where capital works decisions carry the greatest public accountability. That experience shapes every engagement we take on, from initial implementation through to ongoing advisory and Business Intelligence services.

Our Parent Company

Part of HKA Global — A World-Leading Advisory Firm

HKA Tech APAC operates as part of HKA Global — the world's leading claims, disputes, and advisory firm for engineering, construction, and infrastructure. HKA's global network spans 40+ offices on six continents, with deep expertise in capital works claims, dispute resolution, and project advisory.

This connection gives HKA Tech APAC access to a depth of organisational knowledge — including Crux Insight, HKA's proprietary construction intelligence platform — that is unmatched in the regional market. Our local team applies this knowledge directly in Capital Works Advisory engagements and Implementation consulting.
