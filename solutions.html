Plan · Build · Operate

Capital Works Management Across the Full Lifecycle

PMWeb is the specialist choice for organisations managing buildings, infrastructure, and capital programs — a single configurable platform covering the full lifecycle from initial estimating through to long-term asset and lease management.

Phase 01
Plan

Define scope, set baselines, and establish governance frameworks with stage gates and approval workflows. Set program budgets, resource estimates, and contingency at every level of the WBS from day one.
