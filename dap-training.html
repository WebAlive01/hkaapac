WalkMe Walkthroughs & Digital Adoption Tools

HKA Tech is a certified WalkMe deployment partner, helping capital works organisations maximise system uptake and user proficiency — for PMWeb and other enterprise applications. Our digital adoption work is independent of any single vendor or platform.

Digital Adoption

Turning System Deployment into Sustained Proficiency

Software investment only delivers value when users adopt it effectively. HKA Tech's digital adoption practice uses WalkMe and complementary tools to accelerate uptake, reduce support overhead, and maintain proficiency as platforms evolve — across PMWeb and beyond.

1 What is Digital Adoption

In-Application Guidance, Delivered at the Point of Need

Digital adoption platforms (DAP) embed interactive walkthroughs, tooltips, and task flows directly inside software applications — without requiring users to leave the screen or consult separate documentation. For capital works organisations deploying complex platforms like PMWeb, this approach reduces training time, lowers error rates, and sustains proficiency through system upgrades and staff turnover.

Rather than one-off training events, DAP creates a persistent layer of guidance that supports users at every stage of their journey — from onboarding through to advanced workflows.

Contextual walkthroughs Step-by-step in-application guidance triggered at the exact moment a user needs it.
Smart tips and announcements Surface process changes, new features, and compliance reminders without interrupting workflow.
Usage analytics Measure feature adoption rates and identify where users disengage or need additional support.

Beyond PMWeb

WalkMe Across Your Enterprise Application Stack

HKA Tech's digital adoption capability is not limited to PMWeb. We deploy WalkMe across the enterprise applications your capital works teams use every day — delivering consistent guidance wherever your people need it.

3 Multi-Application Deployments

One Adoption Practice Across Multiple Platforms

Capital works organisations typically operate a portfolio of enterprise systems alongside PMWeb — including finance, HR, procurement, and asset management platforms. HKA Tech can deploy WalkMe across any web-based application, giving your teams a consistent guided experience regardless of which system they are working in.

This matters particularly during periods of system change — major version upgrades, new module deployments, or broader digital transformation programs — where users are simultaneously learning new workflows across multiple applications.

Finance & ERP systems In-application guidance for financial approval workflows, budget coding, and reporting processes.
Procurement platforms Walkthrough support for sourcing, tender evaluation, and contract execution processes.
Document management & collaboration tools Adoption support for document control, approval routing, and records management across platforms.

Accelerate time-to-productivity when deploying a new platform. WalkMe walkthroughs activate on day one, guiding users through their first critical workflows before formal training completes.

Version Upgrades

Accessibility Compliance

UserWay — WCAG Accessibility for Your Web Applications

In addition to WalkMe, HKA Tech deploys UserWay to help organisations meet Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) requirements across their web-based platforms. UserWay's AI-powered accessibility widget addresses a broad range of compliance requirements — including screen reader optimisation, keyboard navigation, contrast adjustment, and content scaling — without requiring modifications to underlying application code.
