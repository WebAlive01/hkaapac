The Case for PMWeb

Why Capital Works Owners Choose PMWeb

PMWeb is not a general project management tool repurposed for construction. It is built specifically for owners and operators managing buildings, infrastructure, and capital programs — with a Plan/Build/Operate lifecycle model that reflects how capital works is actually delivered.

Explore the PMWeb Platform
Owner's perspective by design
PMWeb is configured to reflect the owner's view of a capital program — funding, budgets, contracts, documents, and compliance — not the contractor's scheduling view.
Full Plan/Build/Operate lifecycle
A single configurable platform covering estimating through to asset management and lease management — no need to move to a different system at handover.
Configurable to your framework
PMWeb is configured — not customised — meaning HKA Tech can align the platform with PMBOK, PRINCE2, or your organisation's own delivery methodology without bespoke development.
Zero-footprint, true browser access
PMWeb runs from any browser on any device — iPad, iPhone, tablet, or desktop — with no download required. Field teams and project offices access the same system.
