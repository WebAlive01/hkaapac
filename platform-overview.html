PMWeb Platform

PMWeb — The Capital Works Management Platform

Built specifically for owners of buildings and infrastructure, PMWeb brings full lifecycle control — from program planning and procurement through to asset operation — into a single, integrated platform.

Talk to Our Team Explore Platform Features
Built for Owners of Buildings and Infrastructure

Capital Works Management at Every Scale

PMWeb is purpose-built for the owner's perspective, not general project management. Whether the portfolio is a single hospital redevelopment or a ten-year, multi-agency infrastructure program, PMWeb provides the cost control, scheduling discipline, and document governance that capital works demands.

HKA Tech is the #1 PMWeb implementation partner across Asia Pacific, with over 300 organisations trusting PMWeb and our team to run their most complex programs.

Plan

Establish Control Before the First Shovel Turns

The Plan phase gives program directors and procurement teams a single, auditable environment for estimating, approvals, and supply-chain engagement, before construction risk is locked in.

  • Program Planning Define program scope, phasing, funding sources, and approval workflows across the entire capital program from a single workspace.
  • Estimating Build detailed cost models using configurable cost structures, benchmarks, and escalation factors aligned to your organisation's standards.
  • Procurement Manage the full tender lifecycle (RFI, RFT, bid evaluation, and contract award) with complete audit trails required by government panels and accreditation bodies.

Build

Deliver On Budget, On Program

The Build phase puts contract managers, schedulers, and document controllers in one environment, eliminating the email chains, spreadsheet reconciliations, and version-control failures that erode delivery certainty on complex construction programs.

  • Contract Management Track contract values, variations, claims, and payment certifications with full version history and approval audit trails.
  • Scheduling Integrate schedule data with cost and document workflows so program directors see real delivery status, not last week's report.
  • Document Control Manage drawings, specifications, RFIs, and submittals with configurable distribution, review, and approval workflows at project and program level.

Operate

Protect the Asset Long After Handover

The Operate phase ensures the intelligence captured during planning and delivery (contracts, warranties, as-built drawings, maintenance schedules) remains accessible and actionable throughout the asset's life.

  • Facility Management Maintain a living register of assets, spaces, and preventive maintenance obligations linked directly to the original construction documentation.
  • Asset Management Track lifecycle costs, condition assessments, and capital replacement planning across the full asset portfolio.
  • Workflow Automation Automate routine processes (work orders, compliance checks, reporting) to reduce administrative overhead on operational teams.

Hosting & Deployment

Delivered via SaaS. Flexible Where Required.

PMWeb is delivered as a fully managed SaaS solution on Amazon Web Services, giving government and enterprise clients the security, availability, and data-sovereignty assurances their programs demand. On-premise and managed hosting options are also available where organisational requirements dictate.

Recommended

SaaS on AWS

Fully managed, multi-tenant SaaS delivered on Amazon Web Services. Includes automatic updates, infrastructure management, and enterprise-grade uptime commitments. The preferred path for new deployments and existing clients upgrading to PMWeb 8.

Managed Hosting

Dedicated hosting environment managed by HKA Tech on behalf of the client. Suitable for organisations with specific data residency or network isolation requirements that fall outside a standard multi-tenant deployment.

On-Premise

Self-hosted deployment on client infrastructure. Available for organisations with legislative or security requirements that prevent use of cloud or managed services. Implementation and upgrade support provided by HKA Tech.
