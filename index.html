PMWeb Platform

Capital Works Management Across the Full Lifecycle

PMWeb is the specialist choice for organisations managing buildings, infrastructure, and capital programs — a single configurable platform covering the full lifecycle.

Full Platform Overview
Planning

Define scope, set baselines, and establish governance frameworks with stage gates and approval workflows.

Estimating

Set program budgets, resource estimates, and contingency at every level of the WBS from day one.

Procurement

Manage sourcing, tender evaluation, and vendor selection through structured procurement workflows.

Cost Controls

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices and payments across all projects and programs.

Contract Management

Extensive contract management features that dovetail into all areas of PMWeb — cost, schedule, workflow, dashboards and reports in real time.

Program & Project Management

Comprehensive tools for portfolios, programs and projects — configurable to align with PMBOK, PRINCE2 or your organisation's preferred framework.

Mobility

True zero-footprint access — run PMWeb from any browser on iPad, iPhone, tablet or smartphone with no downloads required.

Asset Management

Track, monitor, map and assess locations, facilities, equipment and their subcomponents — with preventive and predictive maintenance, work requests and work orders.

Lease Management

Define and manage cost and revenue leases with sophisticated charge calculations, lease abstracts, suite linking, and direct integration with planning and workflow.
