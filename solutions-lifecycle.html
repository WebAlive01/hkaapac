Capital Works Management
Across the Full Lifecycle

PMWeb is the specialist choice for organisations managing buildings, infrastructure, and capital programs. A single configurable platform that covers every stage — from early planning and budget setting through construction delivery to long-term asset operations.

One Platform. Three Phases.

A Single System From First Budget to Final Asset

Unlike general project management tools, PMWeb is built specifically for capital works owners — the organisations responsible for delivering and operating buildings and infrastructure. Every module is designed for the owner's perspective: cost certainty, contract control, governance compliance, and long-term asset accountability.

Full Platform Overview
Phase 1

Plan

Define scope, establish budgets, and set governance frameworks before a single contract is signed. PMWeb's planning modules give capital works owners the tools to set up programs with the rigour that major infrastructure delivery demands.

Planning
Define scope, set stage gates, and establish approval workflows before delivery begins.
Estimating
Set program budgets, resource estimates, and contingency at every level of the WBS from day one.
Procurement
Manage sourcing, tender evaluation, and vendor selection through structured procurement workflows.

Phase 2

Build

Manage and track funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices, and payments across all projects and programs in real time. PMWeb's Build modules are the operational core for capital works owners during delivery.

[NEW — VERIFY] Phase lead sourced from homepage PBO Build tab content. Client to confirm and supplement with live site copy.

Cost Controls
Manage funding, budgets, contracts, change orders, risks, forecasts, invoices and payments across all projects and programs.
Contract Management
Extensive contract management features that dovetail into all areas of PMWeb — cost, schedule, workflow, dashboards, and reports in real time.
Program & Project Management
Comprehensive tools for portfolios, programs, and projects — configurable to PMBOK, PRINCE2, or your organisation's preferred framework.
Mobility
True zero-footprint access — run PMWeb from any browser on iPad, iPhone, tablet, or smartphone with no downloads required.
Explore Build in Detail
Phase 3

Operate

Once construction is complete, the work doesn't stop. PMWeb's Operate modules allow organisations to transition seamlessly from delivery into long-term asset management — maintaining the data integrity, governance records, and financial accountability built during delivery.

[NEW — VERIFY] Phase lead written from audit and homepage content. Client to confirm and supplement with live site copy.

Asset Management
Track, monitor, map, and assess locations, facilities, equipment, and their subcomponents — with preventive and predictive maintenance, work requests, and work orders.
Lease Management
Define and manage cost and revenue leases with sophisticated charge calculations, lease abstracts, suite linking, and direct integration with planning and workflow.
Facilities & Maintenance
Preventive and reactive maintenance workflows, inspection records, and compliance tracking — all linked back to your delivery data.
