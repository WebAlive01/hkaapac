Analytics & Reporting

Business Intelligence for Capital Works Portfolios

Custom dashboards and reporting that deliver real visibility across your capital works portfolio — for PMWeb and other enterprise systems. An independent HKA Tech capability that generates measurable value with or without PMWeb at the centre.

Contact Us Explore PMWeb
Our BI Capability

Real Visibility. Across Every System.

HKA Tech's Business Intelligence practice builds custom dashboards and reporting solutions for organisations managing capital programs — connecting PMWeb data, financial systems, scheduling tools, and other enterprise platforms into a single, coherent view of performance. Our BI revenue is earned independently of PMWeb: we work with any system your organisation relies on.

PMWeb + BI

Portfolio-Level Dashboards for PMWeb

PMWeb contains a wealth of cost, schedule, contract, and workflow data. HKA Tech's BI team builds reporting layers that surface that data at the portfolio and program level — so executive decision-makers see what they need, when they need it, without navigating individual project records.

  • Executive Portfolio Dashboards Consolidated views of budget performance, contract status, cost-to-complete forecasts, and schedule variance across all projects in a program.
  • Program-Level Cost Reporting Drill-down from portfolio to project to cost-code level, with variance flagging and trend analysis built in.
  • Governance & Workflow Reporting Visibility of approval pipeline status, outstanding actions, and document control metrics — critical for compliance-driven environments.

Enterprise BI

Reporting Beyond PMWeb

HKA Tech's BI capability is not limited to PMWeb. Many capital works organisations operate multiple enterprise systems — financial platforms, ERP solutions, scheduling tools, and asset management databases. We build reporting layers that connect these sources into a unified view, independent of any single vendor.

  • Multi-System Data Integration Connect data from SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, and other platforms alongside PMWeb to produce a single source of truth for program performance.
  • Custom KPI Frameworks We work with your team to define the metrics that matter — then build dashboards that track them consistently across all projects and programs.
  • Reporting for Every Audience From field-level project reports to board-ready executive summaries — each dashboard is designed for its intended audience and decision context.
