Our Approach

A Structured Path from Configuration to Live Operations

HKA Tech's implementation methodology is the product of 25+ years delivering capital works technology across APAC's most complex public and private sector programs. The Implementation Guide documents this approach — not as a generic framework, but as a practical reference for the decisions and milestones your team will encounter.

Configuration and system setup — aligning PMWeb to your program governance structure, workflows, and reporting requirements
Data migration — structured approach to migrating existing project, contract, and cost data into PMWeb without loss of audit trail
Integration with enterprise systems — connecting PMWeb to ERP, finance, and document management platforms already in use
Training and go-live support — role-based training, WalkMe in-application guidance, and hypercare support through the critical first period
